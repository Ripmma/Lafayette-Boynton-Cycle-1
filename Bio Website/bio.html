Saitamas Mind (Rene's Sample Website)

Biography

Saitama is a bald, ordinary looking man with a thin, but well-built physique and of average height and weight. Saitama initially had spiky, black hair, but claims to have lost all of his hair suddenly and prematurely as a result of the toll taken on his body by his intense hero training. Saitama also has brown eyes that are represented as dots.

Abilities

Saitama has superhuman abnormal athleticism that no other has ever surpassed. The origin of Saitama's immense strength is mysterious and unclear. Although he tells everyone he got his incredible power through mere physical training, no one believes him at all. According to Saitama, after a year and half of 100 daily push-ups, sit-ups, and squats, plus 10 km daily running, he had achieved some level of superhuman strength.
